Engineering Project Experience

Capstone Team Leader and Full-System Developer | MICROTECT Automated Optical Detector

Team Leader | April 2026

Developed an AI-integrated optical detection system for identifying, classifying, and quantifying microplastic particles in rock salt for retail and domestic use. The project addressed the time, cost, and repeatability limitations of manual microscopy by combining optical imaging, embedded processing, AI-assisted analysis, frontend result UI, backend processing logic, UI/UX flow, and report generation.

  • Integrated Raspberry Pi 5, HQ camera module, illumination system, display interface, and prototype enclosure.
  • Implemented a YOLOv11 object detection workflow with OpenCV image handling and result visualization.
  • Developed the operator-facing interface and workflow from sample capture to result review, graph views, and report output.
  • Generated analytical outputs including particle count, estimated particle size, MP/g, confidence, and contamination level.
  • Used ADDIE methodology and ISO/IEC 25010 evaluation criteria for development and assessment.
  • Reported 69.71% mean detection consistency, with precision 0.826, recall 0.733, and mAP@50 of 0.814.

MICROTECT Hardware Scope

  • Raspberry Pi 5 processing unit with 64GB SD storage and Pi power supply.
  • HQ camera module, camera stand, LED lamp/ring light, and sample viewing area.
  • 7-inch LCD monitor, sampling containers, Petri dishes, labels, and enclosed prototype body.

MICROTECT Software Scope

  • Image capture and preprocessing for sample inspection.
  • Bounding-box detection with pixel-to-micrometer size estimation.
  • Detection dashboard with histogram/line analysis and PDF-style reporting flow.
