    Capstone project

    Development of an Automated Optical Detector for Microplastics in Rock Salt

    MICROTECT is an automated optical detector for retail and domestic rock salt screening. The system combines optical imaging, a Raspberry Pi-based processing unit, a YOLOv11 AI model, result visualization, and report generation.

    Real-world problem
    Manual microscopy can be slow, inconsistent, and difficult to repeat.
    System approach
    AI-assisted inspection using image capture, YOLOv11 detection, UI feedback, and reports.
    Measured outcome
    69.71% mean detection consistency, with precision 0.826 and mAP@50 of 0.814.
